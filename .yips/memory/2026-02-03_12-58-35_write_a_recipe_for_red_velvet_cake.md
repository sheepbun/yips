# Red Velvet Cake Recipe

This recipe makes a classic red velvet cake with a tender crumb and vibrant color. The key is using a good quality cocoa and buttermilk.

## Ingredients

- **Dry Ingredients**:
  - 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  - 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  - 1/2 cup (100g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  - 1 tsp (5g) baking powder
  - 1/2 tsp (2.5g) baking soda
  - 1/4 tsp (1g) salt

- **Wet Ingredients**:
  - 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk
  - 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  - 2 large eggs
  - 1 tsp (5ml) vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.

2. **Mix dry ingredients**: In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

3. **Combine wet ingredients**: In another bowl, mix buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla.

4. **Combine wet and dry**: Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until just combined (do not overmix).

5. **Bake**: Pour into prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

6. **Cool**: Let cake cool in pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

## Tips for Perfect Red Velvet Cake

- **For deeper color**: Add 1/4 tsp (1g) food coloring (red) to the batter.
- **For moistness**: If cake is dry, add an extra 1-2 tbsp buttermilk.
- **For tender crumb**: Don't overmix the batter - just combine until no dry streaks remain.
